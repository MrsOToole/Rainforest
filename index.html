WELCOME TO RAINFOREST ADVENTURE GOLF!

Located in Dundrum Town Centre are our two indoor, fully air conditioned mini-golf courses suitable for all types of visitors!
we'll provide the clubs and balls and get you playing on our Aztec and Mayan themed 18 hole courses.

The venue is suitable for all ages and skill levels and is fully wheelchair accessible.

“Do I have to book?”
We recommend you pre-book tickets for busy periods such as evenings, weekends and public holidays. Walk in’s are welcome if we aren’t fully booked. Please note card payments only for walk in customers.

“Do you do parties/Groups?”
We are taking groups again. We are Not taking children’s parties. Please visit our groups and events page for details.

“Can I get a refund/move a ticket?”
Tickets can be amended or cancelled up to 48 hours prior to the arrival time. We will not issue refunds to any booking after 48 hours before the booking. To move or cancel a ticket simply use the link on the ticket emailed to you or click here. If you did not redeem your ticket and contact us after the event time/date, no refund or rebooking is available.

“Will it be busy?”
We are typically busy on weekends and bank holidays, we recommend you expect a short queue during these times. Having a ticket guarantees entry but you may still have to queue depending on volume of business.

“Is your venue accessible?”
Very! Both courses are fully accessible for wheelchairs. We have simulated sounds and Lighting in the venue so please note this if it impacts on any visitors you are bringing along. Please be mindful of uneven surfaces on the course.

“Can I bring a buggy on the course?”
We don’t allow buggies on the course, however we will be glad to store them for you.

“Can I bring a packed Lunch/Birthday cake?”
We do not allow any food to be consumed in our venue that is not purchased in our cafe. We do not allow birthday cakes to be brought into the venue.

“Is this for adults?"
Absolutely…we see more adults visit each day than children and are Dublin’s top venue for evening dates!

Can My Child Go in on their own?
We do not allow any child 15 and under into our venue without full adult supervision at all times.
